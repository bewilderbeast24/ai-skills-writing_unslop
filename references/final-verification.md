# Final Verification Criteria

Before finalizing the text, ensure it passes these checks.

## Slop Removal
- [ ] No "magic adverbs" (quietly, deeply, etc.).
- [ ] No "delve", "leverage", or "robust" unless technically essential.
- [ ] No "tapestry" or "landscape" metaphors.
- [ ] No "It's not X -- it's Y" constructions.
- [ ] No rhetorical questions.

## Structural Integrity
- [ ] Sentences vary in length and rhythm.
- [ ] Transitions are logical and organic, not filler.
- [ ] No "Listicles in a Trench Coat."
- [ ] No fractal summaries or signposted conclusions.

## Tone and Style
- [ ] The tone is direct and grounded.
- [ ] No patronizing analogies.
- [ ] No grandiose stakes inflation.
- [ ] Formatting is human-like (straight quotes, simple characters).

## Final Polish
- [ ] Does it sound like a person wrote it in a text editor?
- [ ] Is it free of performative vulnerability?
- [ ] Does it deliver the message efficiently without circular repetition?
